Remember to use dividers when quoting phone numbers or large sums.
 



[[badExample | These number are unwieldy and difficult to read]]
|  1. 2721654230
| 2. Phone: 8583532311


[[goodExample | A comma, a dash and some spacing makes these large digits easier to read]]
|  1. 2,721,654,230
| 2. Phone: (858) 353-2311
