Sometimes you might need to move a rule from a category to another. You can do this by following these steps:

(Steps 1 and 2 can be skipped by clicking on [this link](/_layouts/15/sitemanager.aspx?Source=%7bWebUrl%7d_layouts/15/settings.aspx)​)

1. ​Click on "Site Settings": <br>      <dl class="image"><dt>
            <img src="/Communication/Rules-to-Better-Adding-Rules/PublishingImages/Pages/how-to-move-a-rule/site-settings.png" alt="site-settings.png" style="width&#58;259px;">​ </dt><dd>Figure&#58; Make sure you are logged in and click on &quot;Site Settings&quot;</dd></dl>
2. Under "Site Administration", click on "Content and structure": <br>      <dl class="image"><dt>
            <img src="/Communication/Rules-to-Better-Adding-Rules/PublishingImages/Pages/how-to-move-a-rule/content-structure.png" alt="content-structure.png" style="width&#58;259px;">
         </dt><dd>Figure&#58;&#160;Go to &quot;Content and structure&quot;</dd></dl>
3. Navigate on the categories and pages to find the rule you want to move: <br>      <dl class="image"><dt>
            <img src="/Communication/Rules-to-Better-Adding-Rules/PublishingImages/Pages/how-to-move-a-rule/move-rule.png" alt="move-rule.png" style="width&#58;550px;">
         </dt><dd>Figure&#58; Find the rule to be moved and click on the small arrow beside it, then click on &quot;Move...&quot;</dd></dl>
4. Select the new location where the rule should be: <br>      <dl class="image"><dt>
            <img src="/Communication/Rules-to-Better-Adding-Rules/PublishingImages/Pages/how-to-move-a-rule/select-new-place.png" alt="select-new-place.png" style="width&#58;459px;">
         </dt><dd>Figure&#58; Navigate through categories and click on the place the rule should be moved to​ and click &quot;OK&quot;</dd></dl>
