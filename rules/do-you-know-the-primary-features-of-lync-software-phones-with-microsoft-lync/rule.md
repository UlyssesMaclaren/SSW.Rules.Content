 ​​
 

There are a lot of features of Microsoft Lync. The primary features are:
 
- Internal calls
- Transfer a call
- Conference a call


Plus even more:

- Receiving your voice mails into your email
- Presence Icon
- IM functionality
- Federation Abilities (so you have more people on your IM)
- ​Meetings
- Remote Desktop
- Whiteboard
