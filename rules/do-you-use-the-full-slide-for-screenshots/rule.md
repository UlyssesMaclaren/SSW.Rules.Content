 Small images are hard to see. Remember your audience sitting at the back of the room. Especially for screenshots displaying important text, use all your real estate. <br>
 ![](/PublishingImages/badSmall.jpg)Figure: Bad example - the image doesn't cover the whole slide![](/PublishingImages/goodbig.jpg)Figure: Good example - Cover the whole slide with your image to make it easier for people to see from the back row
