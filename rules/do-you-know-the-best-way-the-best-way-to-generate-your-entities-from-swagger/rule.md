There are 3 ways:
 
1. **AutoR est**  - [https://github.com/Azure/aut orest](https://github.com/Azure/autorest)
2. **NSwagStudio**  - https://github.com/NSwag/NSwag/wiki/NSwagStudio
3. **Swagger Codegen**  - http://swagger.io/swagger-codegen
